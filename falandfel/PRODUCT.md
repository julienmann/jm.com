# Fal & Fel — Product Context

## Product Purpose
A single-page restaurant website for Fal & Fel, a Lebanese and Levantine family-run counter restaurant in Outremont, Montréal (QC), Canada. The site exists to drive foot traffic, online orders (Uber Eats / DoorDash), and catering inquiries. Secondary goal: build community pride and emotional connection around the family story.

## Register
brand

## Users
- Local Montréal residents and Outremont neighborhood regulars seeking authentic Lebanese food
- Delivery customers browsing Uber Eats or DoorDash who land on the site for more context
- Event planners and families looking for catering services
- Students (15% discount is highlighted) and young urban professionals
- Bilingual French/English speaking audience (site is in French; several reviews are in English)

## Brand & Tone
- **Name:** Fal & Fel
- **Founded:** Spring 2024 by siblings Myriam and Michel Kfoury
- **Cuisine:** Lebanese, Levantine, Mediterranean — homemade daily, falafel fried to order
- **Location:** 1344, avenue Van Horne, Outremont, Montréal, QC H2V 1K8
- **Phone:** (438) 380-0918
- **Social:** @falandfel (Instagram, 1,450 followers), Facebook
- **Delivery:** Uber Eats, DoorDash
- **Hours:** Wed–Fri 10h–20h, Sat–Sun 9h–16h, Mon–Tue fermé
- **Tone:** Warm, family-first, authentic, passionate, generous. Not corporate. Feels like being welcomed into someone's home.
- **Tagline:** "Saveurs audacieuses · Ingrédients frais"

## Design System
- **Primary color:** Teal `#1A6B6B` (background)
- **Accent color:** Gold `#F5B831`
- **Dark teal:** `#0F4A4A`
- **Display font:** Lilita One (headings, logo, prices)
- **Script font:** Pacifico (eyebrows, taglines, menu title)
- **Body font:** Nunito (body text, buttons, tags)
- **Aesthetic:** Mediterranean warmth, Moroccan tile motifs, gold-on-teal color story, circular visual elements, gold glow accents
- **Motion:** GSAP with ScrollTrigger, smooth entrance animations, parallax deco, marquee reviews

## Strategic Principles
1. **Family first:** The Kfoury siblings are the product. Always surface the human story.
2. **Convert to order:** Every section should nudge toward ordering (online or in person).
3. **Social proof prominently:** 4.9★ / 262+ Google reviews is a conversion asset — use it everywhere.
4. **Community warmth:** The restaurant is described as "a blessing for the neighborhood" — lean into neighborhood/community language.
5. **Freshness signals:** "Frit à la commande", "fait maison chaque jour" — these are key differentiators vs. fast food competitors.

## Anti-references
- Generic "food delivery app" dark UI
- Corporate restaurant chains (no sterile white / gray / sans-serif minimal)
- Overly trendy glassmorphism or neon aesthetics
- Dense text-heavy layouts without warmth
